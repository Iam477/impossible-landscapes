# OP1 - NPC - Cynthia Lechance [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Cynthia Lechance is the ARTLIFE representative tied to the Macallistar Building. She knows the building’s ordinary paperwork truth: rent, tenancy, maintenance, and the awkward fact that the owner would rather empty the building than preserve it.

## GM Notes
Lechance has no knowledge of the Night Floors or the building’s deeper reality. Her role is to confirm the day-world situation: the tenants stopped paying, no one will speak to her, and ARTLIFE is quietly eager to clear the building for renovation.
