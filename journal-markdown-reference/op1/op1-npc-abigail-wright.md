# OP1 - NPC - Abigail Wright [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Abigail Wright is the missing painter at the center of the case. She was a young artist living in the Macallistar Building, close to her father Thomas Wright and, until her disappearance, still anchored to ordinary New York life. Her apartment now stands as the clearest sign that something impossible happened there.

## GM Notes
Abigail bought a copy of The King in Yellow in February 1995, shared it with the other tenants, discovered the Night Floors in early May, and used them to build her shrine. In the truth of Part One, she is lost to the Night World and has moved toward Carcosa. She cannot be recovered during this operation.
