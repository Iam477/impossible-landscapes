# OP1 - NPC - Michelle Vanfitz [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Michelle Vanfitz is a reclusive writer whose apartment is packed with books. She has stopped attending her writing club, and the only thing about her life that seems organized is the deliberate arrangement of her shelves.

## GM Notes
Abigail’s copy of The King in Yellow ended up on Vanfitz’s shelves after Wright shared it. Vanfitz is one of the clearest routes from the daytime investigation into the Night Floors, because her apartment becomes an access point by night.
