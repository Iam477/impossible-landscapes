# OP1 - NPC - Dr. Marvin Bloom [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Dr. Marvin Bloom, also known to Delta Green as Agent Marcus, is the team’s case officer for Operation ALICE. He is a cautious FBI psychologist turned agent, competent, serious, and committed to keeping his people alive.

## GM Notes
Bloom is part of the newer Delta Green culture of the 1990s. He is not the occult revelation of this chapter; he is the operational spine that gets the Agents into the building and gives them a way to interpret early danger.
