# OP1 - NPC - Carmen Wagner [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Carmen Wagner is Roger Carun’s editor at Crescent Publishing. She does not especially like him, but she knows exactly how his disappearance has wrecked his schedule, his obligations, and her patience.

## GM Notes
Wagner holds the 19 APR answering-machine message and has also been getting strange recurring night calls from an unknown man who says, “Start with Peter Devoras,” before listing names.
