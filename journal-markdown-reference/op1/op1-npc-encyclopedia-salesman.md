# OP1 - NPC - Encyclopedia Salesman [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
The Encyclopedia Salesman is a dashing figure in a pinstripe suit and two-tone shoes, usually seen only in glances as he rushes through doors, across halls, or just beyond where the Agents can quite catch him.

## GM Notes
He is not a stable witness so much as a recurring encounter-form. Agents below higher Corruption mostly catch him only in peripheral glimpses; those who can perceive him more clearly still cannot catch him.
