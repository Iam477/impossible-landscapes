# OP1 - NPC - Louis Post [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Louis Post is a talented illustrator whose professional life has come apart in the last few months. He still carries the look of someone who should be charming, but his apartment and demeanor suggest that charm is now under severe strain.

## GM Notes
Post is less a clue fountain than a pressure point. He is deeply unstable and increasingly dangerous, especially by night.
