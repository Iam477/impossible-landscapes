# OP1 - NPC - Henri de Calvados Castaigne [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Henri de Calvados Castaigne is an aged, courteous, impossible resident of the Night Floors. He shuffles around his rooms in antique clothes, rearranging papers and photographs with the air of a man pursuing a private destiny.

## GM Notes
Castaigne claims Carcosa as his place of origin and dreams of kingship. He is one of the better social bridges deeper into Night World logic, but he is also vain, dangerous, and consumed by bottle- and throne-oriented obsessions.
