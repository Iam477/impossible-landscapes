# OP1 - NPC - David Langford [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
David Langford is the missing cable installer who entered the Macallistar in March and never came back out. If the Agents find him, they find a man who looks less like a repairman than like someone scraped half to death by time itself.

## GM Notes
Langford crossed into the Night Floors on 20 MAR 1995 while working the cable box at the top-floor stair landing just as day turned to night. He has survived by hiding, scavenging old food and drink, and avoiding the other residents.
