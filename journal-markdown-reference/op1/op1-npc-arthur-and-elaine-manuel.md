# OP1 - NPC - Arthur and Elaine Manuel [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Arthur and Elaine Manuel are Thomas’s deeply worried parents. They have been frozen out of their son’s life and are now desperate enough to become disruptive if they think the authorities are hiding something from them.

## GM Notes
Their key value is as external witnesses. On 31 MAR 1995, after dark, they were turned away from the Macallistar by a “man with a suitcase and a large grey dog” who said Thomas no longer lived there.
