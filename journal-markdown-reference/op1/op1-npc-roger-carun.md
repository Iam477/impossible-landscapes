# OP1 - NPC - Roger Carun [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Roger Carun is a moderately successful science-fiction novelist living next door to Wright, across the stairwell. He looks like a man who has not been sleeping, and his apartment feels watched even before anyone can say why.

## GM Notes
Carun is one of the strongest clue carriers in the building. His last outside call went to Carmen Wagner. At night he casually accepts the Night Floors as if they are normal, insists Wright still lives upstairs on Six, and guards his apartment with hairs over openings to detect intruders.
