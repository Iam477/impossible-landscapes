# OP1 - NPC - Thomas Wright [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Thomas Wright is Abigail’s father: a grieving, practical man whose discovery of her transformed apartment turned a private crisis into a police case. He wants answers and has no framework for what those answers may actually be.

## GM Notes
Thomas’s recollections anchor several important facts: Abigail found the Red Book while shopping with him, her mother Ellen died warning him that “He’s coming for my baby,” and Thomas himself found the shrine on 4 JUN 1995. He is emotionally credible, but the truth he carries is larger than he can understand.
