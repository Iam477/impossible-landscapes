# OP1 - NPC - Graham Giuradanda [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Detective Graham Giuradanda is the NYPD detective assigned to the Abigail Wright case. He is charismatic, genuinely kind, and very ready to let the federal Agents take point on the strangest case he has ever seen.

## GM Notes
Giuradanda is one of the safest and strongest allies in Part One. He is grounded, competent, religious, and potentially replacement-Agent material if the table needs him.
