# OP1 - NPC - Thomas Manuel [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Thomas Manuel is a young painter living across the hall from Abigail Wright. By day he is evasive, talented, and unhealthy in hard-to-define ways. By night he becomes one of the clearest windows into the building’s double life.

## GM Notes
Manuel has been drawn deeply into the Night Floors. He recorded Wright’s apartment, hid clues in his plant base, worked in the basement studio, and describes Wright as having “moved on” or “figured it out.” At night he may be found making tapes of Wright’s apartment, working on paintings alone while audibly conversing with someone unseen, or playing ball with the Dog.
