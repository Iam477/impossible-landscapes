# SHARED - NPC - Mark Roark [GM]

## Player-Facing Text
Ragged, profane salesman trapped in a hotel that should not be here. He sounds useless until he says one impossible thing too specifically to ignore.

## GM Notes
Trapped, repeating Night World resident with access to Broadalbin, the Bookshop, and other connected spaces. In Part One he is one of the best ways to push the players toward the larger campaign frame without overexplaining it.
